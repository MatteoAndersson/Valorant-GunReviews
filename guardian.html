REVIEW

Hit headshots and you will succeed !

The guardian is a semi-automatic rifle that's deadly in the right hands. While its slow rate of fire and high recoil can be challenging to handle, its high accuracy and damage output make it a popular choice for those who have the aim to hit headshots. It can however be quite a problem close range with it's slow rate of fire.
