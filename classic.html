REVIEW

Great gun for it's cost !

The classic is a great gun when you want to save up your credits. The low damage and fire rate is to be expected but don't underestimate it's power. If you can hit your headshots it can do some real damage and the secondary fire which shoots three bullets at once makes the gun a real threat up close!
