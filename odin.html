Definition of a spray and pray gun !

The odin is a powerful machine gun that can quickly shred through enemies. With a high rate of fire and a massive magazine, the Odin is a force to be reckoned with. While its high cost and heavy weight can be challenging to work with, skilled players can use it to hold down entire areas of the map. This gun is also great for wall-banging !
