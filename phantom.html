REVIEW

Better than the vandal !

The phantom is one of the most popular guns. It's a fully automatic rifle that's deadly in the right hands. With a high rate of fire, low recoil, and solid accuracy, it's an excellent choice for players who like to push aggressively. This gun if often compared to the vandal because they're so alike and I think the phantom is the better gun of the two.
