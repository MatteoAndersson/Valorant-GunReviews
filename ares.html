Budget Odin !

An affordable light machine gun that's good for suppressing enemies. With a high rate of fire and solid accuracy, it's an excellent choice for those who like to lay down covering fire while their teammates move in for the kill. Its low cost and moderate recoil make it a popular choice for players on a budget.
