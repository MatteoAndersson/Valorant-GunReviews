Budget Operator !

A sniper rifle that's deadly in the right hands. With high damage output and great accuracy, it's an excellent choice for those who like to take out enemies from a distance. One disadvantage of this gun is that it only one shots enemies with headshots. However at the price point of 950 credits it's a force to be reckoned with !
