REVIEW

Great budget spray weapon !

The Frenzy is an affordable option for those who are short on cash but still need a reliable weapon. Although its accuracy and damage output are not the best, it can quickly become a game-changer when you're in a tight spot. With its high fire rate, it's an excellent choice for close-quarters combat. Master the Frenzy, and you'll become a force to be reckoned with on the battlefield.
