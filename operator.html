REVIEW

Most expensive gun for a reason !

The operator is a powerful sniper rifle that can take down enemies with a single shot. While its high cost and slow rate of fire can be challenging to work with, it's an excellent choice for players who like to pick off enemies from a distance. The gun is especially good when paired with an agent with movement abilities.
