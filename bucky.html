Budget Judge !

The bucky is a shotgun that's deadly up close but loses its effectiveness at longer ranges. Its high damage output and low cost make it a popular choice for those who like to get up close and personal with their enemies. However in my opinion the judge is almost better in every way but with almost double the cost.
